### **Custom Analytics in Apigee (Brief)**  

1. **Log Custom Variables**  
   - Use **`MessageLogging`** policy to capture unique data (e.g., `client_type`, `response_size`).  

2. **Export Analytics Data**  
   - **BigQuery**: Stream logs to BigQuery via **Apigee Analytics Hub**.  
   - **SIEM Tools**: Push to Splunk/ELK using **Apigee Webhooks**.  

3. **Custom Dashboards**  
   - Visualize in **Data Studio**/Grafana using exported data.  

4. **Edge JS Tracking**  
   - Inject **JavaScript** in responses to log client-side metrics (e.g., `page_load_time`).
