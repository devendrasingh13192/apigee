### **Evangelizing API-First Design (Brief)**  

1. **Show Business Value**  
   - Highlight **faster integrations**, **reduced costs**, and **scalability**.  

2. **Quick Wins**  
   - Pilot **API-first** for a high-impact project (e.g., mobile app backend).  

3. **Educate Teams**  
   - Workshops on **OpenAPI specs**, **contract-first development**.  

4. **Tooling & Processes**  
   - Adopt **API portals**, **mock servers**, and **CI/CD for APIs**.  

5. **Leadership Buy-In**  
   - Tie API adoption to **revenue metrics** (e.g., partner ecosystem growth).
