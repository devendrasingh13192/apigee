### **Export & Analyze Apigee Analytics (Brief)**  

1. **Export Data**  
   - Use **Apigee Analytics API** or **BigQuery Export** (stream logs to BigQuery).  

2. **Analyze in BI Tools**  
   - **Data Studio/Tableau**: Visualize trends (traffic, errors, latency).  
   - **SQL Queries**: Segment by API/product for ROI insights.  

3. **Custom Metrics**  
   - Track **business KPIs** (e.g., user engagement, revenue per API).  

4. **Alerts**  
   - Set up **anomaly detection** (e.g., traffic drops, error spikes).
