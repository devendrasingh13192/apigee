How would you diagnose and resolve high latency in Apigee proxies? explain in very brief

### **Diagnose & Fix High Latency in Apigee (Brief)**  

1. **Identify the Layer**  
   - **Apigee Trace Tool**: Check proxy execution time per policy.  
   - **Analytics**: Compare `total_response_time` vs. `target_response_time`.  

2. **Common Culprits & Fixes**  
   - **Slow Backend?** → Add caching (`ResponseCache`).  
   - **Heavy Policies?** → Optimize/remove unnecessary logic (e.g., JS).  
   - **Network Latency?** → Use Apigee X (global load balancing).  
   - **Large Payloads?** → Enable compression (`Accept-Encoding: gzip`).  

3. **Advanced Checks**  
   - **External Calls**: Minimize `ServiceCallout`s.  
   - **DNS Lookups**: Cache resolved IPs.  

**Quick Wins**:  
✔ Cache static data.  
✔ Reduce policy steps.  
✔ Upgrade to Apigee X for lower latency.  

**Result**: **Smooth, fast APIs!** ⚡
