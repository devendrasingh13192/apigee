# Common Behavioral Questions

## Leadership & Teamwork:
- "Tell me about a time you had to convince a team to adopt new technology"
- "Describe a conflict you resolved within your team"
- "How do you handle technical disagreements?"

## Problem Solving:
- "Describe your most challenging technical problem"
- "Tell me about a time you failed and what you learned"
- "How do you approach debugging complex issues?"

## Project Management:
- "Describe a project where you had to manage competing priorities"
- "How do you handle scope creep?"
- "Tell me about a successful project launch"
