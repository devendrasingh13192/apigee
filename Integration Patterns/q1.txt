### **API Composition in Apigee (Brief)**  

1. **Aggregate Data**  
   - Use **`ServiceCallout`** to fetch from multiple backends.  

2. **Merge Responses**  
   - Combine JSON/XML payloads with **`JavaScript`** or **`ExtractVariables`**.  

3. **Error Handling**  
   - Partial failures? Return **cached/fallback data** for unaffected fields.  

4. **Cache & Optimize**  
   - Cache sub-requests (**`PopulateCache`**) to reduce latency.
