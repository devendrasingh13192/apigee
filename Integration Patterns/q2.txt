### **Handling Partial Failures in Scatter-Gather (Brief)**  

1. **Timeout & Circuit Breakers**  
   - Set short timeouts per `ServiceCallout` to avoid cascading delays.  

2. **Fallback Data**  
   - Use **cached values** or **default responses** for failed calls.  

3. **Error Flagging**  
   - Return **partial responses** with `error` fields for failed services.  

4. **Async Retries**  
   - Log failures and **retry later** (if critical).  

**Key Policies**:  
- `ServiceCallout` (scatter) + `JavaScript` (merge).  
- `LookupCache` (fallback data).
